# The Weaver (Final Boss)

The original caretaker of the Grand Loom. A towering, many-armed silhouette with threads streaming from every limb.

- Once believed they were granting freedom by shattering the Tapestry  
- Now defends the ruins of the Loom, weaving attacks mid-battle  
- Dialogue is sorrowful and paternal/maternal — sees the player as their “child” or “mistake”  
- True identity left ambiguous: angel? god? mortal who ascended?  
- Fight changes dramatically based on how many Thread Masters you absorbed
