# Thread Master: The Hermit (Blue – Balance)

- Archetype: Isolated guardian of nature and serenity  
- Visual: Cloaked fisherman-monk figure casting glowing blue thread-lines into water  
- Region: Flooded caverns, tranquil coastal temples, rippling reflections  
- Personality: Calm, detached, protective — believes connection is dangerous  
- Choice: Spare → their spouse continues their legacy / Absorb → gain graceful blue movement, world grows colder
